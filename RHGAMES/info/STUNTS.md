# Stunts
*(unknown developer, 1990)*

Tato hra stále čeká na někoho,
kdo jí napíše hezký popis.

MobyGames.

This game is still waiting for someone
to write a nice description. For now,
here's few paragraphs from MobyGames:

Stunts (4D Sports Driving outside of
US) is a 3D car racing simulation that
puts emphasis on racing on stunt
tracks. The game contains a variety of
cars, courses and opponents to choose
from (who represent a difficulty
level). The game provides unusual
stunt objects including loops,
corkscrews and jumps. Race tracks
contain three types of surfaces: paved
road, gravel road and ice. Each of
them offer different grip and
traction. There is also grass and
water that surround roads. The former
slows down the car and the latter is
lethal and the car instantly crashes.
It is possible to watch races in
instant replay from different camera
angles. A track editor is included as
well.

People credited on this game were also
