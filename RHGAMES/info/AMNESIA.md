The protagonist wakes up in a hotel
room in midtown Manhattan with no
clothes and no money, and what is
worse, no memory. Soon he discovers
that some man wants to kill him and he
is wanted for murder in Texas. Now his
quest is to find out what exactly is
going on, who he is and how to set
things right.

The defining feature of Amnesia is the
inclusion of the whole of Manhattan,
more specifically about 4000 separate
locations, 650 streets and the subway
system. For this reason the original
package includes a map and the
"X-Street Indexer" (a code wheel for
finding the nearest cross street when
aligning the avenue name with the
street address number), so that the
player can find his way.
