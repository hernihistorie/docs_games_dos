You're playing a small ant that is
willing to help a friend in a rather
inconvenient situation. Apparently,
the Quavers mascot Colin Curly likes
his Quavers so much he lost balance
and dropped them down a giant
ant-hill. It's now your job to go in,
in a mission to solve puzzles and
retrieve the snacks for your friend.

Now, this ant-hill is a bit different
from the ones you saw, and especially
from the one you live in. Its path is
filled with domino-like tiles and
closed gates that must get through to
find the snacks. You'll have to
traverse different themed worlds in
order to retrieve Colin's precious
Quavers.
