# Blockout
*(unknown developer, 1989)*

Tato hra stále čeká na někoho,
kdo jí napíše hezký popis.
Prozatím je zde pár odstavců z
MobyGames.

This game is still waiting for someone
to write a nice description. For now,
here's few paragraphs from MobyGames:

Blockout is a Tetris variant with an
added twist: it's in 3D. The player
can rotate the blocks in 3D in order
to fit them into the pit they are
being guided into. Advanced users can
play this game with odd
three-dimensional pieces, or change
the size of the pit, for depth of
between 6 and 18 piles, and width and
length of between 3 and 7.

People credited on this game were also
credited on:

https://www.mobygames.com/game/498/blockout/
