Worms (¬ervi in Czech) is a game for
2-6 players simultaneously, were all
the players control their worms on the
same screen. From the very beginning,
each worm is increased in length with
same speed in certain direction. By
default, player should not collide
with other worm bending its increase
to the left or to the right. However,
the gameplay options may be adjusted
to allow certain types of collisions.
Players may be organized in teams.
Different amount of points is assigned
to the players based on the type of
collision. The winner is a first
player who reaches the final score
defined before the match start.
