WarCraft II: Tides of Darkness is a
fantasy themed real-time strategy game
and sequel to WarCraft: Orcs & Humans
set on the world of Azeroth. With the
conquest of the kingdom of Azeroth in
the first war, the orcs are now
preparing for an invasion of Lordaeron
and conquer the remaining human,
dwarven and elvisch realms. In an
effort to counter this invasion the
humans, elves and dwarves formed an
alliance in the hope to avert the same
fate the kingdom of Azeroth met.

The game comes with many new features
such as fighting not only over land
but also over sea and air, SVGA
640x480 resolution graphics with many
newly-rendered buildings and units,
multiplayer-support with up to eight
players, as well as a Red Book audio
soundtrack, and a scenario editor that
allows players to design and create
their own scenarios. The editor
requires Windows 3.1 or greater, even
though WarCraft II is a DOS-based
game.
