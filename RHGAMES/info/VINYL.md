# Vinyl Goddess from Mars
*(unknown developer, 1995)*

Tato hra stále čeká na někoho,
kdo jí napíše hezký popis.

MobyGames.

This game is still waiting for someone
to write a nice description. For now,
here's few paragraphs from MobyGames:

In the year 200 billion a small ship
races across the galaxy taking The
Vinyl Goddess From Mars to the
esteemed intergalactic B Movie
convention. In mid transit, a meteor
shower strikes without warning and the
ship is engulfed in a sea of rocks and
debris. Badly damaged by the cosmic
storm, the ship careens off course.
Desperately, The Vinyl Goddess twists
knobs and pulls levers to regain
control. The best that she can hope
for is to eject and let the ship crash
land on the strange planet below. It's
up to you to help the lovely goddess
find and repair her ship and collect
all of her belongings before it's to
late to reach the convention.

Vinyl Goddess From Mars is a platform
game similar in design and gameplay to
the older Jill of the Jungle, or
alternatively Gods. As Vinyl, you must
progress through levels and episodes
and eventually repair your broken
vessel.
