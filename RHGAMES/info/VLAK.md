# Vlak
*(page., 1993)*

Tato hra stále čeká na někoho,
kdo jí napíše hezký popis.
Prozatím je zde pár odstavců z
MobyGames.

This game is still waiting for someone
to write a nice description. For now,
here's few paragraphs from MobyGames:

Vlak (translated as Train from Czech)
is a freeware 90's clone of Snake. You
control a train to collect items
scattered around an area while
avoiding to crash into something.
Every time you obtain a new item, your
train will have a new wagon attached
to it. The wagons differ in looks
based on what kind of item you pick
up. The pickups include animals,
crowns, diamonds, plants and apples.
When you collect all the items, you
can exit through a yellow door to
advance to a new and even more
complicated room. There is an
unlimited amount of continues and no
highscore, but there are 50 levels
while the program weighs only 13 KB.

In addition to this game, these people
were also credited on other games:

https://www.mobygames.com/game/20297/vlak/
