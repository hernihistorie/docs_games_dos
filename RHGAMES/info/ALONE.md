Jeremy Hartwood has committed suicide
in his Louisiana mansion, Derceto. You
take the role of either Edward Carnby,
a private investigator, or Emily
Hartwood, Jeremy's niece, and
investigate the suspicious death. The
three-floor mansion is reputed to be
haunted by its eccentric past owner.
Very quickly, you realise that it is.
Warped rats, zombies, and giant worms
are among the foes who are after you,
and you must somehow escape.

Alone in the Dark is an
action-adventure survival horror game.
The action is displayed from a number
of fixed viewpoints. The backgrounds
are 2D painted images, while the
player character, all items, and
monsters are rendered as 3D models.
