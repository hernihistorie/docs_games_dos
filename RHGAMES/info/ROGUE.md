# Rogue
*(unknown developer, 1980)*

This game is still waiting for someone
to write a nice description. For now,
here's few paragraphs from MobyGames:

Rogue is a turn-based dungeon crawler
in which the player controls an
adventurer who must explore the
dangerous Dungeon of Doom in order to
retrieve the precious Amulet of Yendor
and make it out alive. The player
character starts on the upper-most
level and slowly makes his way
downwards.

The game uses ASCII characters to
represent locations, items, monsters,
and the protagonist himself. There are
twenty-six different types of
monsters, symbolized by their initial
letters (e.g. L for Leprechaun).
Monsters have different abilities and
modes of attack. The dungeon and the
items in it are randomly generated
each time the player begins a new
game. Each dungeon level contains a
grid of three by three rooms and dead
ends.

https://www.mobygames.com/game/1743/rogue/
