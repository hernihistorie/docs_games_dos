Lemmings is a single-player puzzle
game in which players guide groups of
creatures through hazardous
environments by assigning them
specific functions/abilities.

Each level begins with a group of
lemmings dropping into a
side-scrolling environment. These
creatures march forward blindly,
turning only when they hit a wall, and
will walk into hazards such as pits,
water, lava, or traps unless directed
otherwise. The player's goal is to
save at least a set percentage of the
lemmings by leading them to the
level's exit. Levels vary in design,
with some requiring the rescue of only
a handful of lemmings and others
demanding careful planning to save
large groups under tight time limits.
