Caleb was once a feared gunslinger,
until he met a woman who was involved
with a mysterious cult known as Cabal.
Caleb, too, is sucked in, and soon
becomes one of the Chosen - the most
favored worshippers of the evil god
Tchernobog. But one day, Tchernobog
inexplicably kills the four Chosen,
including Caleb. Mysteriously
resurrected years later, Caleb is out
to get revenge, and learn why his lord
cast him down so abruptly.

Blood is a first-person shooter with
horror elements, which uses the Build
engine. Similarly to Duke Nukem 3D,
the environment is interactive: many
objects can be physically moved or
affected in other ways. Destruction of
landscapes is possible (although only
in specific spots), with bullet holes
being left by misplaced shots. The
Build engine version is 2.5D, so there
are rooms above rooms and puzzles
which are based on this.
