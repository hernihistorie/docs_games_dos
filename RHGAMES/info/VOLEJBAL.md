Arcade Volleyball is a simple,
two-player (human or computer)
volleyball-like game where you have
to, simply enough, bang the ball so
that it hits the opposite player's
ground. Some basic rules apply: when
the ball's current owner loses a round
(either because the ball touched the
ground or he touched it for three
times) the opposite player becomes the
owner. If a player is not the owner of
the ball and loses a round, the
opposite player gains a point. The
game is over when either player
reaches 15 points.
