This game is still waiting for someone
to write a nice description. For now,
here's few paragraphs from MobyGames:

Micro Machines 2: Turbo Tournament is
the second game in the Micro Machines
series. Like its predecessor, it puts
the player behind the steering wheel
of an extremely small car. The races
take place in locations such as a
table, a treehouse, and many more.

The CD-ROM version adds CD-audio music
while the Mega Drive (Genesis) version
features a special cartridge that
allows players to connect two
additional controllers to the console
for four-player games.
